Frequently Asked Questions

Food and Otherwise

1. Is your food fully organic?
Due to the fact that most of our ingredients are locally sourced our options can often be limited. Due to this we have ensured that every product has a detailed description giving information about the types of ingredients used.
2. Where is the service available?
Currently due to our small size, we are primarily serving a small community, as we expand we hope to allow sending of our food over larger distances without comprimsing quality. As a company the quality of our food is whats more important to us, we will not venture into that aspect of the company untill we are sure we can do it right.
3. I was told the first meal is free, but I have yet to see any such offer. Why?
we are dumbfounded by the shear frequency at which we have gotten this question. We request that customers stop asking this question for it is slowing down the limited resources we have for customer service.
